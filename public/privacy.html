Privacy

Last updated: May 2026

What this site does

SharedAnime lets you enter public MyAnimeList or AniList usernames to find anime everyone has completed. We do not ask for passwords or account logins.

What we collect

  • Usernames you type, sent to our server so we can load public lists from MyAnimeList and AniList.
  • Basic server logs: your hosting provider may log IP address, browser type, and request time (standard web server stuff).

We do not run user accounts and we do not intentionally store your search results or usernames in a database after the request finishes.

Third parties

When you search, we contact:

  • MyAnimeList (public list data)
  • AniList (public list data via their API)
  • Image CDNs used by those sites for anime posters

Those services have their own privacy policies. We are not affiliated with MyAnimeList or AniList.

Rate limits

To avoid overloading external services, we limit how many searches you can run per minute from the same connection.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on the project repository or contact whoever operates this site.
